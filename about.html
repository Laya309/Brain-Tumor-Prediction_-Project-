About Our Project

This project is a Deep Learning-based web application that helps in early detection of brain cancer using MRI scans. By leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), the model can identify and classify different types of brain tumors such as glioma, meningioma, and pituitary, as well as detect normal (no tumor) scans.

Our goal is to assist medical professionals and patients in getting a faster, more accurate preliminary diagnosis, which can lead to timely treatment and better outcomes. The system is designed to be easy-to-use, secure, and accessible to all.
